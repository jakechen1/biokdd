Held with ACM SIGKDD 2017 · Aug 13–17, 2017
Theme: Multiscale and Multimodal Analysis for Computational Biology

Workshop overview

BIOKDD 2017 encourages KDD researchers to take on the numerous challenges that Bioinformatics offers. This year's edition features the theme of Multiscale and Multimodal Analysis for Computational Biology — the use of data mining and machine learning approaches for the analysis of large amounts of heterogeneous complex biological and medical data being generated. Deep-learning methods are particularly encouraged. The goal is to build accurate predictive or descriptive models from these data, enabling novel discoveries in basic biology and medicine.

Selected high-quality papers from the workshop are invited to a special issue of IEEE/ACM Transactions on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (TCBB), continuing the BIOKDD tradition.
