BIOKDD, 2004

4th Workshop on Data Mining in Bioinformatics

August 2004
Seattle, Washington, USA
in conjunction with
10th ACM SIGKDD International Conference
Proceedings of the 4th ACM SIGKDD Workshop on Data Mining in Bioinformatics — edited by Mohammed J. Zaki, Shinichi Morishita, Isidore Rigoutsos.
Theme: Open call — first transition in chair leadership

Program — Accepted Papers

10 contributed papers (6 long + 4 short) out of 26 submissions (38.5% acceptance) · 1 keynote · 4 sessions

Long papers

  1. A New Approach to Protein Structure Mining and Alignment — Hongyuan Li, Keith Marsolo, Srinivasan Parthasarathy, Dmitrii Polshakov (Ohio State)
  2. A Novel Approach for Prediction of Protein Subcellular Localization from Sequence Using Fourier Analysis and Support Vector Machines — Zhengdeng Lai, Yang Dai (UIC)
  3. Meta-classification of Multi-type Cancer Gene Expression Data — Benny Fung, Vincent Ng (HK Polytechnic)
  4. Bayesian Model-Averaging in Unsupervised Learning From Microarray Data — Mario Medvedovic, Junhai Guo (U Cincinnati)
  5. Discovering Spatial Relationships Between Approximately Equivalent Patterns in Contact Maps — Keith Marsolo, Hui Yang, Srinivasan Parthasarathy, Sameep Mehta (Ohio State)
  6. Differential Association Rule Mining for the Study of Protein-Protein Interaction Networks — Christopher Besemann, Anne Denton, Ajay Yekkirala (NDSU)

Short papers

  1. High-throughput Protein Interactome Data: Minable or Not? — Jake Chen, Andrey Sivachenko, Lang Li (Indiana U / Prolexys Pharmaceuticals)
  2. Assessment of Discretization Techniques for Relevant Pattern Discovery from Gene Expression Data — Ruggero Pensa, Claire Leschi, Jeremy Besson, Jean-Francois Boulicaut (INSA / INRA Lyon)
  3. Clustering Labeled Data and Cross-Validation for Classification with Few Positives in Yeast — Miles Trochesset, Anthony Bonner (U Toronto)
  4. A Maximum Entropy Approach to Biomedical Named Entity Recognition — Yi-Feng Lin, Tzong-Han Tsai, Wen-Chi Chou, Kuen-Pin Wu, Ting-Yi Sung, Wen-Lian Hsu (Academia Sinica)
