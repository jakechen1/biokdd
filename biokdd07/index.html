BIOKDD, 2007

7th Workshop on Data Mining in Bioinformatics

August 12, 2007
Fairmont Hotel, 170 South Market Street, San Jose, CA, USA
in conjunction with
13th ACM SIGKDD International Conference (KDD'07)
Proceedings of the 7th ACM SIGKDD Workshop on Data Mining in Bioinformatics — edited by Mohammed J. Zaki (general), Jake Y. Chen, Stefano Lonardi.
Theme: First full-day workshop — open call on biological data mining

Program — Accepted Papers

10 contributed papers (7 long + 3 short) · 1 keynote · 4 sessions · full-day workshop

Long talks

  1. Gene Selection by Matrix Reordering and Replicator Dynamics — Wenyuan Li, Xiuwen Zheng, Ying Liu (UT Dallas / U Washington)
  2. Investigating the Use of Extrinsic Similarity Measures for Microarray Analysis — Duygu Ucar, F. Altiparmak, H. Ferhatosmanoglu, Srinivasan Parthasarathy (Ohio State)
  3. Mining Over-Represented 3D Patterns of Secondary Structures in Proteins — Matteo Comin, Concettina Guerra, Giuseppe Zanotti (U Padova / Georgia Tech)
  4. Combining Domain Fusions and Domain-Domain Interactions to Predict Protein-Protein Interactions — Nguyen Thanh Phuong, Tu Bao Ho (JAIST)
  5. A Linear-time Algorithm for Predicting Functional Annotations from Protein-Protein Interaction Networks — Yonghui Wu, Stefano Lonardi (UC Riverside)
  6. Profile-feature based Protein Interaction Extraction from Full-Text Articles — Shilin Ding, Minlie Huang, Hongning Wang, Xiaoyan Zhu (Tsinghua U)
  7. A Decomposition Approach for Discovering Network Building Blocks — Qiaofeng Yang, Stefano Lonardi (LBNL / UC Riverside)

Short talks

  1. Use of Gene Ontology as a Tool for Assessment of Analytical Algorithms with Real Data Sets — Megan Kong, Zhongxue Chen, Yu Qian, Jennifer Cai, Jamie Lee, Eva Rab, Monnie McGee, Richard H. Scheuermann (UT Southwestern / SMU)
  2. Clustering of Non-Alignable Protein Sequences — Abdellali Kelil, Shengrui Wang, Ryszard Brzezinski (U Sherbrooke)
  3. Discovering Ovarian Cancer Biomarkers using Gene Ontology Based Microarray Analysis — Wei Guan, Alexander Gray, Sham Navathe, Nathan Bowen, John McDonald, Lilya Matyunina (Georgia Tech)
