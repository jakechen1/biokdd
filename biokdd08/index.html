Publication

ACM Digital Library workshop proceedings. Selected papers in IEEE/ACM TCBB special issue — first year of the TCBB tradition. Workshop site originally at IUPUI (defunct).

BIOKDD 2008 began the TCBB special-issue tradition that has continued every year since. The accompanying 733-page book Biological Data Mining (Chen & Lonardi, eds., Chapman & Hall/CRC, Sept 2009) collected expanded versions of papers from 2007 and 2008.
